Gender Equity

Striving for Equality: Empowering All Genders

What

What is Gender Equity

Gender Equity means provision of fairness and justice in the distribution of benefits and responsibilities between women, men and all genders.

Why

Why is Gender Equity important

Women aren't equal anywhere. It'll take 170 years worldwide for things to change. That's bad for our sons too, affecting everyone's economy.

How

How can we Create Gender Equity in Surrounding

To create gender equity, we need to educate, challenge stereotypes, and provide equal opportunities. Empower women, address bias, and promote inclusive policies to make our surroundings more fair and equal for everyone.
